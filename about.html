Who We Are

HYGGE is a lifestyle brand focused on comfort, style, and well-being. We offer a carefully selected range of products that help create a cozy, peaceful atmosphere in your home and daily life. From candles and home decor to furniture and personal care items, each piece is chosen to inspire relaxation and a sense of contentment. At HYGGE, we believe that the right environment can enhance your overall well-being, providing a sanctuary for the mind, body and soul.

Our Story

HYGGE is a Danish concept that defines a state of comfort, warmth, and well-being, often associated with the small, pleasant moments of life. It’s about creating a relaxing, intimate atmosphere where you feel at peace and content. More than a style, it is a philosophy of life that promotes well-being and emotional connection with the space and people around you. The founders of the store, while searching for furniture for their own home, realized that many of the design brands they admired were not locally available. Driven by the desire to fill this gap and to bring high-quality design into the spotlight, they decided to create this store.

Design Therapy

It has been scientifically proven that interior design plays a therapeutic role, and the space around us can influence our mood. Whether it's furniture, lighting fixtures, decorative objects, or anything else that can tell your story, delight your eyes and soul with what you love! For us, every product is more than just a simple functional object meant to fill an empty space. We focus on sensations, pleasures, and emotions that you experience when you look at and touch the new furniture pieces. You notice colors, feel textures, and sense perfect shapes.

Exclusive Brands

We understand that every customer is unique, and that makes them special. That's why we have added a wide and diverse range of brands to our portfolio that never fail to surprise us with furniture, decorations, lighting fixtures, and many other products. We want shopping at HYGGE to be a true experience, where you feel special and surrounded by unique products. That's why we've managed to add a series of exclusive brands to our portfolio: Kave Home, Wood, Nordal, Zuiver, Versmisen and others.
