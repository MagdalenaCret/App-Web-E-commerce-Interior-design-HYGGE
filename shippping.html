Shipping and Returns

What are the available delivery methods and costs?
↓

We provide the following delivery methods:

  • Courier Delivery: €10
  • Easybox Delivery: €5

For orders with cash-on-delivery payment, an additional processing fee of €1.5 is charged.

What is your return policy for defective products?
↓

If the ordered product doesn't match the description or has manufacturing defects, you can request a replacement or refund within 30 days from receipt. You can make this request:

  • Directly from your customer account using the "Return" option
  • Via our return form link
What are my legal rights regarding returns?
↓

Under Article 9, paragraph 1 of Government Ordinance No. 34 of June 12, 2014, you have the right to withdraw from distance contracts without providing a reason within 14 days from receiving your products. To exercise this right:

  • Send an unequivocal statement via email
  • Note that products purchased from physical stores cannot be returned
How does the refund process work?
↓

Upon withdrawal, we will refund any amount received within 14 days of being informed of your decision. Refunds are processed as follows:

  • Standard orders: Via bank transfer to your provided account
  • Online card payments: Refunded to the original payment card
How do I return my products?
↓

Send products to:

123 Furniture Street, Design District, New York, NY 10010

  • Products must be returned within 30 days of receipt
  • You are responsible for the direct cost of returning products
  • Products must be in original condition and packaging
  • You are only liable for value diminishment beyond what's necessary to establish nature, characteristics, and functionality
What about vintage and handmade items?
↓

Please note:

  • Decorative items in vintage style or handmade items may have small imperfections (minor scratches or material flaws)
  • These are not considered manufacturing defects
  • Warranty conditions cannot be invoked for these characteristics
  • You can still request a return without explanation, in accordance with the law
