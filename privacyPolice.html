Privacy Policy

This privacy policy informs you about the information we collect from you when you use our website. By collecting this information, we act as a data controller, and under the relevant U.S. data protection laws and regulations, we are obliged to provide you with information about us, the purpose and manner of using your data, and the rights you have regarding your personal information.

How do we use your information?

When you use our website to browse our products and services and view the information we provide, a number of cookies are used by us (first-party cookies) and third parties (third-party cookies) to ensure the website functions, collect useful information about visitors, and offer you the best possible experience when using our site.

When you submit a request via our website, we ask for your name, contact phone number, and email address.

We use this information to respond to your inquiry, including providing information about our products and services. We may email you several times after your inquiry to ensure your question has been answered and to improve our user experience.

Emails from inquiries are retained for two years, after which they are securely archived for five years and then deleted. CRM records are kept for three years from your last contact with us.

When you buy products from our website, we will ask for the following information:

1. Your name;

2. Billing details;

3. Address;

4. Contact phone number;

5. Delivery address;

6. Email address;

7. Bank and IBAN code for bank transfer payments.

We use this information to process your order and deliver the requested products. Additionally, we will email you an invoice and may use your phone number to contact you about your purchase.

This information is required to process payment, deliver products or services, and fulfill your request.

We do not use the information you provide to make automated decisions that could affect you.

Your rights as a data subject

You may ask us what information we hold about you and request corrections if it is inaccurate. If we have requested your consent to process your personal data, you can withdraw this consent at any time.

If we process your personal data to fulfill a contract or based on your consent, you may request a copy of your information in a readable format for transfer to another provider.

If we process your personal data based on consent or legitimate interest, you can request the deletion of your data.

Finally, in certain circumstances, you can request that we refrain from making decisions that affect you through automated processing or profiling.

To submit a request about your personal data via email, post, or phone, use the contact information provided above in the "CONTACT" section.

Updates to this privacy policy

We regularly review and update this privacy policy as needed. If we intend to use your personal data in a way not identified previously, we will contact you to inform you about it and, if necessary, request your consent.
