Company Policy

At HYGGE, we strive to maintain a positive and productive work environment, ensuring mutual respect, professionalism, and integrity in all aspects of our operations. This policy is designed to communicate our core values, expectations, and the standards to which we hold our employees, clients, and partners.

Equal Opportunity and Non-Discrimination

We are an equal opportunity employer. We value diversity and are committed to providing a workplace free from discrimination, harassment, or intimidation based on race, gender, age, religion, sexual orientation, disability, or any other characteristic protected by law.

Code of Conduct

Employees are expected to conduct themselves with honesty, integrity, and respect for others. Professional behavior and collaboration are essential to achieving company goals and maintaining a positive work culture. Disrespectful conduct, including harassment, bullying, or unethical behavior, will not be tolerated.

Confidentiality

Employees must maintain the confidentiality of sensitive company information, including financial data, trade secrets, and client information, both during and after their employment. Breaches of confidentiality may result in disciplinary action.

Workplace Safety

The safety of our employees is a top priority. All employees are expected to follow safety guidelines and report any unsafe conditions or practices. [Company Name] complies with all relevant safety regulations and strives to create a work environment that promotes the health and well-being of all employees.

Attendance and Punctuality

Employees are expected to maintain regular attendance and arrive at work on time. If unable to attend work due to illness or personal circumstances, employees must notify their supervisor as soon as possible. Unexplained or excessive absences may result in disciplinary action.

Use of Company Resources

Company property and resources, including computers, phones, and software, should be used for business purposes only. Misuse of company resources for personal gain or non-work-related activities is prohibited.

Social Media and Public Representation

Employees should use social media responsibly and respect the company’s reputation. Employees must refrain from posting or sharing content that could be viewed as harmful to the company or its stakeholders. When representing HYGGE, employees should always act professionally, both online and offline.

Discipline and Termination

If employees feel they have been treated unfairly or have a concern regarding any aspect of their work environment, they are encouraged to speak with their supervisor or HR department. HYGGE is committed to resolving conflicts in a fair and professional manner.

Amendments

HYGGE reserves the right to amend or update this policy at any time. Employees will be informed of any changes, and the revised policy will take effect upon notification.
