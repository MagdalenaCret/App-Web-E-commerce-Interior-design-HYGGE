Payment Methods

We offer you the following payment methods:

Cash on Delivery

The cash-on-delivery payment is made in full upon order delivery. If the products are delivered by couriers, payment will be made to the agent handling the delivery.

Bank Transfer (Payment Order)

After placing the order, you will receive all the necessary information to include on the payment order. The order will be delivered only after payment confirmation. To expedite the process, please contact us and send a copy of the payment order.

Online Payment - PayU

You can make an online payment with your personal or company card, in complete safety. Card payments are secure because your card details will not be stored by the processor; they are encrypted and protected. To make the payment, you will need to enter your card number, expiration date, and CVV2/CVC2 code (the last three digits printed on the strip on the back of the card, next to your signature).
